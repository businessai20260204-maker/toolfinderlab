## What Are AI Video Tools? (And Why Beginners Get Confused)

If you've searched for AI video tools before, you've probably noticed something strange: the results are all over the place. That's because "AI video tools" is actually an umbrella term covering two very different types of software.

**Type 1: Avatar-Based Video Tools**
These tools create videos featuring a realistic AI presenter‚Äîa digital human who speaks your script. You type your text, choose an avatar, and the tool generates a professional-looking spokesperson video. No filming required. Tools like Synthesia and HeyGen fall into this category. They're ideal for business explainers, training videos, and corporate communications.

**Type 2: Text-to-Video Generators**
These tools transform written prompts or scripts into fully generated video scenes‚Äîthink cinematic footage, animations, or social media clips created entirely by AI. Tools like Runway and InVideo fall here. They're better suited for creative content, social media, and marketing campaigns.

**Why does this matter for beginners?**
Most comparison articles mix both types together, which creates confusion. A small business owner looking to create training videos has completely different needs from a content creator wanting viral TikTok clips. Knowing which category you need makes choosing your tool ten times easier.
